Endgame (2022),
performance and installation, Geneva (CH)

A staged reflection on the backstage, not as a preparation, but as its own suspended reality. Endgame unfolds in the liminal space before or after the so-called "main event", where performers slip into half-formed characters, waiting, reharsing, or remembering.

Collaborators were invited to inhabit invented roles, creating a fragmented chorus of presence, anticipation, and post-performance residue.

With Emy Curti, Zeltia Robin and Alice Oechslin
