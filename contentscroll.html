Scroll (2022),
video in collaboration with Azadbek Bekchanov, Alexandra Salem and Quyen Lignier, Amsterdam (NL)

Digital fanzine of moving poems. Three invited writers contribute texts adapted into short video formats. Fragments, loops, and reflections that exist somewhere between poetry and feed.

Scroll contains «Aerith» by Azadbek Bekchanov, «A goddess know she is pure light cuz she transformed shadows (1)/extract» by Aalexandra Salem and «Lettre à Alexandre» by Quyen Lignieres.
