4all (2023),
performance and sound installation, Academie voor Theater en Dance, Amsterdam (NL)

A moving installation rooted in medieval aesthetics and sonic experimentation. At its core the question: «How do swords sing?», Built into a live electronic setup generating noisy, metallic textures that conjure digital anachronism.

with Yooha and Xiao Chuyue
Music by Christian Johannes and Marike Knegtering
Documentation by Nellie De Boer
