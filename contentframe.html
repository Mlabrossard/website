Frame (2024),
performance and video installation,
in collaboration with Ulysse Berdat, Veem House, Amsterdam (NL)

An horror film staged for theater and live recording.
Three dancers rehearse in an empty space. They’re not alone. As the invisible presence grows, so does the question: can they escape?
