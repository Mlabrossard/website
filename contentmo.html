Mo (2025),
performance, Vlaams Cultuurhuis de Brakke Grond, Amsterdam (NL)

A participatory dance work built from mosh pits, punk concerts, and LARPing.
Using gaming structures and DIY dramaturgy, Mo invites performers and audiences into a chaotic, communal choreography of impact and play.

[Watch video]
